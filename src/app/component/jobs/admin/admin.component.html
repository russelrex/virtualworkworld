Logistics & Operations Administrator

Client’s Profile

The company is a US-based Asian-American family business established in the 1970s with a mission to make Asian foods more accessible and elevate the experience of enjoying Asian cuisine. They currently offer a range of premium-quality snacks, sauces, and other packaged goods that are crafted using only the finest ingredients and innovative production methods. Their incredible products are found online or in Publix, Wegmans or Hy-vee. Their team is dedicated to creating community and inclusivity through their products and brand. Join them as they continue to share the rich flavors and traditions of Asian cuisine with the world.

What you’ll do

This company seeks a highly motivated Logistics & Operations Administrator to join their team. The ideal candidate will coordinate freight, work with suppliers, create SOPs, manage projects, and handle procurement and logistics specifics. You will work closely with the CEO and are expected to provide exceptional administrative support. Other responsibilities for this role include but are not limited to:

  • Coordinate with freight and cost per unit.
  • Coordinate and monitor supply chain operations
  • Working with Partners & Distributors: Emailing, Follow-ups, Deadline adherence & coordinating sales samples
  • Provide sales communications for collections and funds.
  • Manage relationships with vendors, manufacturers, and other partners to ensure timely delivery of goods and services.
  • Monitor inventory levels and implement processes to ensure adequate supply while minimizing waste and excess inventory, activate order management, provide forecasting help, and provide monthly inventory reports.
  • Analyze data to identify areas for improvement and implement process improvements to increase efficiency and reduce costs.
  • Assist in the audit of documentation and contract terms required for trade transactions.
  • Provide receipt of products based on procurement requirements and quality assurance processes.
  • Track statuses of purchase orders based on organizational procedures.
  • Collaborate with other departments to ensure supply chain operations align with overall business goals.
  • Bookkeeping tasks, including invoicing and follow-ups
  • Organizing Google Workspace and electronic filing systems
  • Drafting Standard Operation Procedures, as needed
