Timeline

*Before the final interviews, your team will be sent 'Candidate Portfolios,' including each candidate's resume, a video introduction, references feedback, interview notes, security clearance, completed assessments, and all relevant details.

  • Week 1

    We review all porfolios and pull ten vetted Virtual Admin Assistants from our pipeline that best matches your specific job description.

    We conduct preliminary interviews, bringing it down to three candidates. We do all reference checks and security verifications.

  • Week 2

    The three candidates then go through two thorough interviews and are asked to complete a skill-based assessment.

  • Week 3

    Two finalist candidates will be chosen and brought for interviews* with the hiring company.

  • Week 4

    A hiring decision is made and one candidate is onboarded!
