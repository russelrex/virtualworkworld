  • Don't risk hefty fines and legal issues when it comes to policies for your offshore contractors. Let our specialist team create internationally compliant policies that are tailored to your company's culture.

  • With our polished policies in place, you can focus on building strong relationships with your offshore team and growing your business.

Founded on fairness

We put people first.

Unlike other remote hiring platforms, we don't make any money off upcharges, or take a slice of contractor earnings.

Why? Because it's the right thing to do, and it's the best thing for your business too.

We believe that when you treat business relationships with as much care and respect as all other relationships, you both reap the rewards.

We help create compatible working relationships and remote workplace policies that bring out the best in each other and help businesses boom.
