Why we'll work for you

Hiring Timeline

  • Week 1

    We help you draft your job description, then get to work on finding you the best match in our Pipeline of vetted talent. We handpick the best people for the job based on your keywords, then review all the applications and choose ten candidates to move forward. We then interview each candidate and narrow it down to the top three finalists. We carry out all background, character reference and security checks.

  • Week 2

    The top three candidates then go through an in-depth interview with the Lead Recruiter, where they also complete a skills-based assessment to see if they're a good fit. After this assessment, they have a final interview with the Managing Director.

  • Week 3

    We then choose two final candidates, who will be sent for interviews with your company. Before the interviews, you'll be sent Candidate Portfolios which includes a profile on the candidate with their resume, a video introduction, references, interview notes, security clearance and completed assessments.

  • Week 4

    Here's where you make your hire! Once you've made the decision, it's time to get them onboarded. And we can help with this too. Just take a look at our other services.
