Jisselle Baldwin-Todd

Hey! It's great to have you here. I'm Jisselle, the Founder of Virtual Work World, and I work remotely from the Caribbean.

The remote recruitment world is a horror scene. Culture clashes, missed potential, and recruitment companies that take a percentage of employee earnings.

A business relationship needs the same foundation as any relationship. Connection, compatibility and a commitment to do the best for one another.

I've worked in the corporate world for 17 years, and I've seen some things that have made me sad (and squirm!).

An overworked workforce, undervalued teams, a lack of processes and worst of all, injustice. It's even worse for remote workers, and that's not a world I want to live in. That's why I founded Virtual Work World, to give remote workers, rights.

I've worked across Customer Success, Recruitment, Marketing and Digital Growth for over 15 years, led global support teams and marketing for award-winning brands and consulted for many companies internationally, so I know how vital it is for businesses to get the best talent and tech as their key pillars of strength. I have built and managed teams in Brazil, El Salvador, India, the Philippines, Jamaica, Trinidad & Tobago, Mexico, the US, the UK, and Canada. With my 14 years’ experience in scaling global teams, I not only find your talent, but match you with the best candidate to suit your business needs and budget.

Nothing makes me happier than helping others achieve their career dreams (that, and time spent with my three quirky children).

But a company is only as good as the team behind it, which is why I want you to meet mine.
